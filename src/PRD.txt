Requirements:
- Staking contract that only accepts ETH
- ETH is automatically converted to WETH
- Receipt(DMitongToken-WETH) tokens are minted to the depositor
- Reward token is DMitongToken at an APR of 14%
- Rewards are calculated based on the proportion of ETH deposited
- Users can opt in for auto-compounding
- Auto-compounding fee is 1% of WETH deposited
- Auto-compounding can be triggered by anyone externally
- Person who triggers auto-compounding gets a reward
- Reward comes from the total auto-compounding fee of people in the pool
- Withdrawals can be instant
